Description

You can serve up 10 tacos in just 30 minutes with this simple taco recipe

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salsa
  • Taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomato
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef over medium heat 8-10 minutes while stirring occasionally until brown
  2. Stir salsa into beef. Heat until boiling while stirring then reduce heat to medium-low. Cook for 5 more minutes then poor into bowl
  3. Heat taco shells according to instructions
  4. Serve tacos with ground beef and desired toppings
