Description

This at-home pizza recipe is bound to taste better than takeout. You shoudln't have to rely on takeout places for great pizza. Today we will show you how to make great tasting pizza, that i relatively cheap and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Store bought dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pizza toppings

Steps

  1. Heat oven
  2. Form pizza dough and place it on dusted with flour or cornmeal
  3. Slip pizza onto the hot stone
  4. After 5 minutes of baking, check pizza
  5. When the cheese is melted to a medium-to-dark brown, the pizza is done
