Description

Cheeseburgers are a staple when it comes to American culture! This recipe is quick, easy, and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Choice of seasoning
  • Shredded cheese & sliced cheese
  • Hamburger buns
  • Choice of toppings (onions, tomatoes, mayo, lettuce)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  2. Hand mix hamburger, seasoning, and shredded cheese in mixing bowl
  3. Hand form patties to desired size
  4. Grill hamburgers. Flip when patties no longer stick
  5. Once flipped put cheese on top of each patty
  6. Assemble burgers with desired toppings
